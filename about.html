About

Born in 2002 in Mumbai, India.
I believe that Engineering can be used to tackle and consult on problems of all walks and domains of life. With a mission to understand these problem statements and to brainstotm their solutions... I am pursuing new expressions through experiments and thoughts.

View My Works

I have always been a curious student, constantly asking questions about how things, especially machines, work. This curiosity motivated me to secure an MHT-CET rank of 786, allowing me to join the Mechanical Engineering Department at VJTI for a 4-year B.Tech program. VJTI's rich culture and numerous committees exposed me to various fields, including Management, Core applications, and Art.

These committees allowed me to explore various domains and excel in real-world scenarios. I progressed from assisting superiors in minor tasks to designing and organizing my own events, eventually leading the entire organization as Secretary. This role involved hosting one of Asia's largest technomanagerial events, managing a crowd of around 50,000 people over three days.

This interest in management led me to a 2-month internship at Glatt Systems Pvt. Ltd., where I learned how management and core engineering collaborate for organizational success. I then worked on data analysis and IoT projects for my final year, which fueled my passion for IoT, AI, and Data Analytics. This experience helped me transition into the professional world, joining ICICI Bank as a product manager in their International Banking Department.
